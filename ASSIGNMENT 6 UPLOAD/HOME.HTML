Who Are They?
   BTS also known as the Bangtan Boys, is a seven-member South Korean boy band formed by Big Hit Entertainment. 
The name subsequently became a backronym for Beyond the Scene in July 2017. On June 12, 2013, they performed
 the song "No More Dream" from their initial album 2 Cool 4 Skool to commemorate their debut on June 13, 2013.
